Top 5 K-Dramas of 2024

Alaa Salah

K-Drama Fan & Blogger

Alaa has been reviewing K-dramas for 5 years, sharing insights and recommendations for drama lovers.

2024 has been an amazing year for K-Dramas, with incredible storylines, outstanding performances, and breathtaking cinematography. Here are the top 5 K-Dramas of the year that you shouldn't miss!

1.Queen of tears

"Queen of Tears" is a highly anticipated 2024 K-Drama that tells the emotional yet heartwarming story of a chaebol heiress and her husband, who face unexpected challenges in their marriage. Starring Kim Soo-hyun and Kim Ji-won, the drama blends romance, drama, and comedy, captivating audiences with its deep storytelling and stunning cinematography.

2.Love next door

"Love Next Door" is a heartwarming romantic comedy that follows the story of two neighbours who fall in love despite their contrasting personalities.The drama explores themes of love, friendship, and finding happiness in unexpected places, offering a light-hearted and relatable story that will leave you with a smile.

3.Judge from the hell

"Judge from the Hell" is a dark and thrilling drama that explores the themes of justice, revenge, and the human condition. The story centers around a group of individuals who seek to punish those who have escaped the justice system, taking the law into their own hands. The drama features compelling characters and a gripping plot, keeping viewers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

4.MR Plankton

The series stars Woo Do-hwan as Hae Jo, a man ostracized due to his unconventional birth, and Lee Yoo-mi as Jo Jae-mi, a woman facing her own personal challenges. The plot centers on Hae Jo, who, upon learning he is terminally ill, compels Jae-mi to accompany him on a final journey to find his biological father.

5.When the phone rings

The series stars Yoo Yeon-seok and Chae Soo-bin as a couple in an arranged marriage who receive a threatening phone call from a kidnapper, leading them into a suspenseful and emotional journey.
